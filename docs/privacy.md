# Privacy policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This is my personal website. I want to be upfront about what happens when you visit.

## What I collect

I use cookies to remember your preferences (like dark mode or cookie consent) and to understand how people use the site. That's it. I don't collect personal information, email addresses, or anything I could use to identify you.

## Analytics

I use anonymous analytics to see which pages get visited and how people find the site. This helps me figure out what content is useful. The analytics don't track you across other websites and don't collect personal data.

## Cookies

The site uses a small number of cookies:

- **Consent cookie** - remembers whether you accepted or declined cookies
- **Analytics cookies** - only set if you consent, used for anonymous usage stats

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

## Third-party services

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information (like IP addresses) as part of serving the site. See [GitHub's privacy statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) for details.
